To fix this glitch goto settings, click video, then click edit,
and turn your shadow quality to at least the medium preset.

Why is my name not changing?

If you are in the Multiplayer or Forge lobby, back out to the main menu and select either Multiplayer or Forge.

Then you name is changed!

How do I change teams in the Multiplayer Lobby?

Use the Q and E keys on the keyboard to switch between teams.

How to I backup maps and gametypes I made?

You select the SAVE MAPS AND GAMETYPES option on the mainmenu to save them to your computer.

Press any key to close this window.
